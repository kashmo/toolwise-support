ToolWise Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

What We Collect

ToolWise stores only the tool data you enter — names, brands, models, locations, photos, and notes. This data is saved on your device and optionally synced to your private iCloud account.

What We Don't Collect

ToolWise does not collect analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers, or any personally identifiable information. There are no third-party SDKs, trackers, or ad networks in the app.

iCloud Sync

If iCloud is enabled, your tool data syncs to your private iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit. Only you can access this data. Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy governs how Apple handles that data on their servers.

Photos

Tool photos are saved to your device and to your private iCloud account so they survive uninstall and appear on your other Apple devices. Photos are stored as encrypted assets within your iCloud account and are not uploaded to any third-party server.

UPC Product Lookup

When you scan a barcode, ToolWise sends the UPC number to a product lookup service to retrieve the product's name, brand, model, and image. Only the UPC number is transmitted — no other tool data, no personally identifiable information, and no device identifiers. Lookup results are cached in your iCloud account so repeat scans of the same product do not re-query the service.

Camera & Microphone

Camera access is used only for scanning tool boxes and taking tool photos. Microphone access is used only for voice entry. Both are requested on first use and can be revoked in iOS Settings at any time.
