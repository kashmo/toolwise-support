Privacy policy

Your tools are your business.

Last updated: July 2026

ToolWise is designed to help you catalog and find your tools while collecting as little information as practical.

No ToolWise account

ToolWise does not require you to create a separate ToolWise account. The app uses Apple technologies available on your device.

Inventory and photo storage

Your tool inventory and photos are stored locally and may sync through your personal Apple iCloud account using CloudKit. Apple’s terms and privacy practices govern iCloud.

Barcode and product lookup

When you scan a barcode, the UPC may be sent through a Cloudflare Worker to a third-party product database to look for matching product information. ToolWise may cache product lookup results to improve speed and reduce repeat requests. Do not place personal information in barcode fields.

Camera, microphone, and speech recognition

ToolWise requests camera access for barcode scanning and tool photos. It requests microphone and speech-recognition access when you use voice entry. These permissions are requested only when needed and can be managed in iOS Settings.

Data sales and advertising

ToolWise does not sell your personal data and does not use your inventory to build an advertising profile.

Support communication

If you email support or submit a beta request, the information you choose to provide is used to respond to your request and manage beta participation.
